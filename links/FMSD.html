Attendance and Grading

There is no mandatory attendance for the lecture or lab, however, note that the final grade includes your activity during the semester.

The final grade is computed as follows: FG = 20% * M1 + 10% * M2 + 40% * E + 20% * L + 20% * P + 10% * B, where:

  • M1 - grade from Midterm 1
  • M2 - grade from Midterm 2
  • E - grade from Final Exam
  • L - grade from Lab
  • P - grade from Projects
  • B - grade from Bonuses

If FG > 4.1 it is 5, > 5.1 it is 6, ..., > 9.1 it is 10.

For more details see Fisa Disciplinei (IR 2), respectivelly Syllabus (IE 2).
