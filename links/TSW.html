Grading

In order to be able to attend the exam you need to:

  • fulfill the requirements listed above (Mandatory attendance)
  • prepare 2 incremental versions (1 page, respectively 5 pages) of your bachelor thesis (other project) in which you follow the advices on how to write papers given in the lecture. Additional materials: below.

The grade is computed from the scores you obtain in the written exam (max. 70 points) and the activity in the seminar during the semester (max. 40 points).

91 points (or more) means 10, 81-90 points means 9, ..., 41-50 points means 5, so you need 41 points to pass the lecture.

Even if you have no activity at the seminar you can pass the lecture, however, the grade will be lower.

Your activity at the seminar will be graded on a scale from 1 to 10.
