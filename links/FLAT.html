Attendance and Grading

There is no mandatory attendance for the lecture or seminar, however, note that the final grade includes your activity during the semester.

The final grade is computed as follows: FG = 30% * M + 40% * E + 30% * S + 20% * P, where:

  • M - grade from Midterm
  • E - grade from Final Exam
  • S - grade from Seminar, where 10% is the individual project on the evaluation of an arithmetic operation and 20% homeworks, tests, etc.
  • P - grade from Projects

Remark: Students which have an innovative idea and implement it which is close to the topics of the lecture are able to skip the final exam. In this case, they are required that until Week 7 to contact the teacher from the lecture, present their idea (problem specification, short description of how the solution will be tackled), and then each second week to present the advances of their project. The project is individual. If this option is chosen, then the student can not choose also 10.6 Projects.

If FG > 4.1 it is 5, > 5.1 it is 6, ..., > 9.1 it is 10.

For more details see the Syllabus.
