Attendance and Grading

There is no mandatory attendance for the lecture or lab, however, note that the final grade includes your activity during the semester.

The final grade is computed as follows: FG = 30% * M + 20% * E + 60% * L, where:

  • M - grade from Midterm
  • E - grade from Final Exam
  • L - grade from Lab activity

If FG > 4.1 it is 5, > 5.1 it is 6, ..., > 9.1 it is 10.

For more details see the Syllabus. The homeworks are optional but they help you preparing for the Midterm and Final Exam.

Optional Homeworks

  • Use a SAT solver for the problems introduced in Course 4 (Modelling with Propositional Logic).
  • Use a SMT solver for solving the Virtual Machine Placement Problem from Course 11 - part 2 (encodings Variant 2 - 4).
