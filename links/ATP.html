Grading

In order to be able to attend the exam you need to fulfill the requirements listed above (Mandatory attendance).

The grade is computed from the scores you obtain in the written exam (max. 70 points), the activity in the seminar during the semester and programming projects (max. 40 points).

90 points means 10, 80 points means 9, ..., 40 points means 5, so you need 40 points to pass the lecture.

Even if you have no activity at the seminar you can pass, however, the grade will be lower.

Your activity at the seminar will be graded on a scale from 1 to 10 for each action (homework, presentation, etc.).
